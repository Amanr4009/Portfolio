About Me

Full Stack Developer

Hello! I'm Aman Kumar, a dedicated individual with a passion for problem-solving and a deep-rooted love for technology. My journey into the realm of technology commenced during my college, where I cultivated a robust foundation in programming languages like Python, Java, and C. As I delved deeper into my studies, I delved into various facets of computer science, encompassing data structures, algorithms, machine learning, and artificial intelligence.Throughout my academic tenure, I actively engaged in projects that provided hands-on experience in applying theoretical knowledge to real-world scenarios. From crafting web-based code editors to spearheading full-stack social networking applications, I've had the privilege to work with an array of technologies including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React.js, Node.js, MongoDB, and more. In addition to my academic pursuits, I've garnered invaluable industry exposure through internships, such as my stint at Habitat for Humanity. Here, I honed my skills in front-end and back-end development, alongside mastering database management systems. Outside of my academic and professional pursuits, I'm constantly seeking ways to broaden my skill set and deepen my knowledge. My certifications in Full Stack Development and Front-End Development attest to my dedication to continuous learning. As I look ahead to the next phase of my journey, I'm brimming with anticipation to push the boundaries of technology further, unravel intricate problems, and make a tangible impact in the dynamic realm of software engineering.
